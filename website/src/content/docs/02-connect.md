---
title: Connect to PostgreSQL
description: Add your first database connection securely.
order: 2
---

1. Open SQLTemple and click **Connect** in the toolbar.
2. Fill the form:
   - **Name**: Friendly label
   - **Host**: e.g., `db.example.com`
   - **Port**: `5432` (default)
   - **Database**: target database name
   - **Username** and **Password**
   - **SSL**: enable if your host requires TLS
3. Save. Your credentials are stored locally with platform keychain support.
4. Use the schema explorer to browse databases, schemas, tables, and columns.
